My Work Work

I am a Developer Advocate, specializing in cloud database technology, developer productivity, and community organizing. I recently fell in love with the Offline First community. My Community Work

I founded Girl Develop It Burlington in 2013, and currently I work with my co-leader to manage an 4-person volunteer team focused on providing learning and community resources to women who want to learn how to code.
I co-organize the UX Burlington conference, which is a two-day, single-track conference-style gathering, focused on what it really takes to build a great user experience on the web.
I am an Executive Fellow for BTV Ignite.

My Blog

My love affair with writing began in Ms. Cardomom's class in 6th grade. In my blog you'll find musings from my personal experiences and rantings about society at large. I'm interested in learning more about how to fight for those who don't have a voice, supporting underserved communities, and chronicling my adventures living in the frosty North.
